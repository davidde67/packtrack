## Phase 1 — "Get Growing" (April 2026) | Budget: ~$2,500
### Construction Sequence (order matters!)
1. Clear and level entire 21×35.5 ft area
2. Mark all 6 bed positions with stakes + string (even if only 4 built in Phase 1)
3. Lay heavy-duty landscape fabric over entire area
4. Place raised bed frames on marked positions
5. Fill pathways with 2-3" of 3/4" minus gravel (~7 cu yds)
6. Fill beds using hugelkultur method
7. Install basic drip irrigation
